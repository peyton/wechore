Support

WeChore Support

For app issues, feedback, or feature requests, email support@wechore.peyton.app. Privacy questions go to privacy@wechore.peyton.app.

Households

Start with your name and household name. Add members with optional FaceTime or phone handles for voice and text handoff.

Chores

Add a chore, choose who owns it, set a due date, and update progress from open to done.

Suggestions

WeChore suggests chores only from messages typed inside WeChore. Review each suggestion before adding it.

Reminders

Local notifications require Apple notification permission. Text and voice nudges open Apple system apps.

CloudKit sharing

Shared households use Apple iCloud infrastructure and each member's Apple ID.

Security

For security reports, email security@wechore.peyton.app.

Before emailing support

  • Include your iPhone or iPad model and iOS version.
  • Describe what you expected and what happened instead.
  • For sync questions, note whether iCloud is enabled on the device.
  • For reminder questions, note whether notifications are allowed in Apple system settings.

General contact: contact@wechore.peyton.app.
