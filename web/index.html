Shared Household Chores

Turn the house chat into a clear chore list.

WeChore lets households assign work, check progress, send local reminders, and review chore suggestions from messages typed inside the app.

Ask about WeChore Read privacy policy

Built for the work after someone says it.

Chores, reminders, and due dates stay visible without a WeChore-owned account or server.

Assign and track

Create chores, choose an assignee, add due dates, and move work from open to done.

Suggest from messages

On-device parsing can turn an in-app message into a chore suggestion for review.

Use Apple handoff

Reminders can use local notifications, Messages, FaceTime Audio, or Phone.

Local-first by default.

WeChore uses SwiftData on device and CloudKit sharing through each member's iCloud account. It does not include ads, analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
